# Changelog

## [1.0.4](https://github.com/bitMuse-Ostranauts/SmarterHauling/compare/v1.0.3...v1.0.4)

### Features

* NPCs (not in your crew) will periodically recharge their EVA oxygen and batteries, preventing them from suffocating to death
* NPCS and PCs (in your company) will seek out EVA chargers to swap their batteries when they run low.
* NPCS and PCs (in your company) will seek out O2 bottles in haul zones to swap out their bottles when they run low.
* NPCS and PCs (in your company) will remove their helmet if the atmosphere is breathable and their suit no longer has battery or O2 remaining.

## [1.0.3](https://github.com/bitMuse-Ostranauts/SmarterHauling/compare/v1.0.2...v1.0.3)

### Bug Fixes

* Depositing items into containers now requires line of sight (no more teleporting items through doors and walls)

### Features

* Backpacks, specialty totes, toolboxes, and smart crates are now capable of having storage preferences set on them.
* Being interrupted from our container drop now produces an aLog.
